Refund Policy

Hassle-free refunds on eligible products

Overview

At Watchory, we are committed to ensuring that your shopping experience is smooth and satisfactory. If you are eligible for a refund, we process it as quickly as possible.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of receiving the product.
  • Products must be in original condition, unused, and with all packaging, tags, and accessories intact.
  • Proof of purchase (invoice or order confirmation) is required.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Customized, engraved, or personalized watches.
  • Products that show signs of wear, damage, or mishandling.
  • Final sale or clearance products.

Refund Process

  • Once we receive the returned product, we inspect it to ensure it meets the refund criteria.
  • Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the product was damaged or incorrect.

Damaged or Incorrect Products

If you receive a defective or wrong item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost.

Contact Us

For any refund-related questions, please reach out to us at: support@watchory.com
