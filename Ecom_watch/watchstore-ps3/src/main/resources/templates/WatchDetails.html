Description

A watch is a portable timepiece, worn on the wrist as a wristwatch or carried in a pocket as a pocket watch, used to tell time and often considered a fashion accessory. Watches can be mechanical, powered by a wound spring, or quartz, powered by a battery-driven vibrating crystal. Features and specifications include the type of glass used, like mineral or sapphire crystal, and water resistance ratings.
