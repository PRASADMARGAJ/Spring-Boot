Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters — here’s how we protect it.

1. Introduction

Watchory ("we", "us", "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the choices you have. By using our website or services you accept the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account details: name, email, phone number, shipping & billing addresses.
  • Order information: products purchased, order history, payment method (we do not store full card data).
  • Communications: messages, feedback, support requests.

2.2 Information we collect automatically

  • Device & usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL, timestamps.
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Cookies section).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill orders, manage payments, and provide customer support.
  • To send transactional messages (order confirmations, shipping updates) and marketing communications if you opt-in.
  • To improve our website, analyze trends, and personalize your experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud/misuse.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We may share information with:

  • Service providers: payment processors, shipping carriers, hosting providers — strictly to perform services for us.
  • Legal requests: when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified).

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember preferences, analyze site usage, and serve relevant content. You can control or disable cookies via your browser settings, but some features may not function if cookies are blocked.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you suspect a security breach, contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When data is no longer needed it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object to certain processing. You may also have the right to portability of your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have such data, contact us and we will remove it.

10. International Transfers

Personal data may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure that transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards.

11. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated policy with the effective date. Continued use of our services after changes means you accept the new policy.
