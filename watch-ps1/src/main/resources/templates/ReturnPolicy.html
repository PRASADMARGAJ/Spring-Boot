Return & Refund Policy — Watchory

Return & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority

Overview

At Watchory, we are committed to providing premium quality watches and exceptional customer service. If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help.

Return Eligibility

  • You may request a return within 7 days of delivery.
  • The watch must be unused, unworn, and in original condition.
  • All original packaging, tags, and accessories must be included.
  • Proof of purchase (invoice or receipt) is required.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Watches with scratches, signs of wear, or physical damage.
  • Customized or engraved products.
  • Final sale or clearance products.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned watch, our quality team will inspect it. After approval:

  • Refund will be initiated within 5–7 business days.
  • Refund will be processed to your original payment method.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable.

Exchange Policy

You may exchange your watch for a different model of equal or higher value. Price difference (if any) must be paid by the customer.

Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the product received is damaged or incorrect.

Damaged or Wrong Product

If you receive a defective or incorrect watch, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund.

How to Request a Return

  1. Email us at support@watchory.com
  2. Provide your order ID, phone number, and reason for return.
  3. Our team will guide you through the return process.

Thank you for shopping with Watchory.
