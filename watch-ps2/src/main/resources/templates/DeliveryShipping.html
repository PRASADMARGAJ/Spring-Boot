Delivery & Shipping Policy

Fast, Secure, and Reliable Delivery

Shipping Overview

At Watchory, we strive to deliver your luxury timepieces quickly and safely. All orders are processed with the highest care to ensure on-time delivery and secure packaging.

Order Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within 24–48 hours (excluding Sundays & public holidays).
  • You will receive an email confirmation once your order is shipped.

Estimated Delivery Time

  • Metro Cities: 2–4 business days
  • Other Cities & Towns: 4–7 business days
  • Remote or Rural Areas: 7–10 business days

Shipping Charges

  • Free shipping on all prepaid orders.
  • COD orders may include an additional fee of ₹49–₹99 depending on delivery location.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can monitor the entire journey of your package online.

Delivery Partners

We work with trusted logistics partners such as:

  • Delhivery
  • Blue Dart
  • DTDC
  • India Post Speed Post

International Shipping

We currently offer delivery only within India. International shipping will be available soon.

Failed Delivery Attempts

  • Your package will be held for 3 days.
  • Our team will contact you for re-delivery.
  • Additional charges may apply for re-shipping.

Damaged Package

  • Take photos of the package immediately.
  • Do NOT accept the delivery if the seal is broken.
  • Contact our support team within 24 hours.
